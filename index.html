Donate Your Bike

How to Donate Your Bike:

Simply enter Phoenix Bikes, head up to the counter, and say "I'm here to donate my bike." Our team will help you to fill out a quick form for your receipt and get your bike ready to be processed.

What We Accept

Category We Accept: We do NOT accept:
Bicycles • Bikes in rideable or repairable condition
• Youth bikes (16" wheels or larger)
• Mountain bikes (hardtail or full suspension)
• Road bikes & touring bikes
• Hybrid & commuter bikes
• Single-speed & fixed-gear bikes
• Bikes with damaged frames but good components 		• Excessively rusted bikes
• Bikes missing major parts (forks, proprietary parts)
• Bikes with seized or frozen parts
• Low-quality department store bikes (Roadmaster, Huffy, Magna)
• Tandems, 3-wheel bikes
Tires & Tubes • New or gently used tires & tubes
• All sizes (12"– 29" tires)
• Presta or Schrader valve tubes 		• Dry-rotted, cracked tires
• Bald tires with no tread left
• Tires with large cuts or holes
Wheels • Good condition (no major dents or warping)
• Wheels with minor wobble or loose spokes
• Rim brake or disc brake compatible wheels
• Wheels with hubs in good condition
• 26", 700c, and 29" wheels 		• Severely bent or warped wheels
• Cracked or dented rims
• Wheels with missing hubs or axles
• Wheels with completely rusted spokes
Bike Parts • Drivetrain Components
• Braking Systems
• Handlebars & Stems
• Cables & Housing
• Headsets & Forks 		• Damaged or excessively worn parts
• Heavily rusted or seized parts
• Non-bike-specific parts
Accessories • Bike locks with combination code or key
• Lights & Reflectors
• Kickstands
• Bells & Horns
• Mirrors & water bottle cages
• Handlebar tape & grips
• Saddles & handlebar bags 		• Used water bottles
• Broken or non-functional locks
• Dead or non-working lights
• Expired helmets
• Non-bike related accessories
Tools & Gear • Bike-specific tools
• Repair stands
• Lubricants & Cleaners
• Protective gear (gloves, safety glasses, aprons)
• Cycling gloves and socks 		• Broken or damaged tools
• Dirty, dried-out lubricants or cleaners
• Non-bike-specific tools or gear
• Cycling jerseys, bibs, or other apparel
Other Items • Workshop supplies and consumables
• Cleaning supplies (hand cleaner, rags & shop towels, spray bottles, brushes, sponges) 		• Car bike racks
• Stationary/exercise bikes/bike trainers
• Hazardous materials
• Non-bike-related equipment
